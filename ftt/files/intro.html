What is FTT?

FTT is a computer CTF (capture the flag) which means you try to find
"flags" using your computer skills.

It stands for Find The Thing because we aren't feeling very creative.

We would recommend you have knowledge in batch files
and a basic knowledge in computers.

Participating is completely free and anonymous.
It is targeted towards middle schoolers from grades 5 to 8 with windows computers.
